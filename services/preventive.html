Preventive Dentistry

Stopping problems before they start.

The Foundation of a Healthy Smile

Preventive dentistry is the practice of caring for your teeth to keep them healthy. This helps to avoid cavities, gum disease, enamel wear, and more.

Key Preventive Measures

  • 1. Fluoride Varnish: A quick, painless application that strengthens enamel and fights early decay. Highly recommended for children every 6 months.
  • 2. Pit and Fissure Sealants: A protective coating applied to the deep grooves of back teeth (molars) to seal out food and bacteria, preventing cavities.
  • 3. Diet Counselling: Expert guidance on nutrition and snacking habits to reduce sugar intake and acid attacks on teeth.

Routine Hygiene

Professional cleanings (scaling) remove hard tartar that brushing can't reach. We recommend a visit every 6 months to maintain optimal oral health.
