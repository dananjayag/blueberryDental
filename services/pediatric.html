Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle care for your little ones.

Why Choose a Pediatric Dental Specialist?

Children are not just small adults. Their dental needs change as they grow. At Blueberry Dental, we specialize in making children feel safe and comfortable.

Our Core Pediatric Treatments

  • 1. Fillings: Gentle removal of decay and restoration with tooth-colored materials to keep the tooth functional and aesthetic.
  • 2. Pulpectomy (Root Canal for Baby Teeth): A specialized procedure to save a deeply infected baby tooth, preventing premature loss and ensuring space for the permanent tooth.
  • 3. Crowns: Stainless steel or zirconia crowns to fully protect and restore extensively damaged baby teeth.

Pulp Therapy (Pulpectomy)

Sometimes a baby tooth has a deep cavity that reaches the nerve. A pulpectomy is a gentle procedure to remove the infection and save the tooth, ensuring proper space for the adult tooth to grow.
