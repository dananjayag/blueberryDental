Habit Corrections

Breaking habits for a lifetime of healthy smiles.

Why Correct Oral Habits?

Childhood habits like thumb sucking or mouth breathing can significantly affect the growth of the jaw and the alignment of teeth. Early intervention is key to preventing complex orthodontic issues later in life.

Common Habits We Treat

1. Thumb Sucking

Prolonged thumb sucking can push teeth forward and reshape the roof of the mouth.

2. Mouth Breathing

Breathing through the mouth can lead to dry gums, increased cavities, and narrow jaw development.

3. Lip Biting / Nail Biting

These habits can chip teeth and cause stress on the jaw joint (TMJ).
